Make Chocolate

This program calculates the amount of chocolate bars of 2 different sizes(kilos) needed to match the goal amount of kilos. It also determines if the amount of bars you have can meet the goal, and subtracts the difference for each order of chocolate. This project is where I learned how to push to heroku.
